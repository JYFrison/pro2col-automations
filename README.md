## 🎯 About This Project
This repository serves as a public library of advanced automation templates, AI system prompts, and integration architectures designed specifically for health, wellness, and coaching professionals. 

Our goal is to make enterprise-level data processing and AI-driven health protocols accessible to independent practitioners, allowing them to scale their impact without needing a dedicated IT team.

## 📂 What's inside?
*(Repositories are currently being migrated and updated for the US Hispanic and EU markets)*

- **`/n8n-workflows`**: Blueprint JSON files for connecting CRMs (like GoHighLevel) with external APIs and AI models.
- **`/ai-agents`**: Master system prompts for specialized AI assistants (longevity research, health protocol generation, client onboarding).
- **`/scripts`**: Utility scripts for VPS deployment and webhooks management.

## 🚀 Why Claude AI?
The workflows presented here are highly dependent on processing large datasets of scientific research and complex health protocols. We are actively transitioning our core NLP tasks to **Claude**, leveraging its superior context window and nuanced reasoning capabilities to ensure the highest quality of outputs for our practitioners.
